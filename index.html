The problem
01

Recorded — just not quantified

CVs, résumés, LinkedIn profiles — they list what you did, not what it was worth. The value you created is invisible, even to the people deciding your next salary.

02

Visibility beats value

The people who speak loudest get promoted. The people who do the work get overlooked. This isn't a personality problem — it's a systems problem.

03

History travels. Value doesn't.

A résumé gets you in the room. It rarely wins you the argument. Without verified, quantified evidence of what you've delivered, you're negotiating on trust — not proof.

How Eva works

Your record. Built by you. Impossible to ignore.

Step 01

Capture your contributions

Step 02

Step 03

Step 04

Pay rise. Promotion. New role. Your verified contribution record means you're no longer asking to be believed — you're presenting evidence. That changes the dynamic entirely.
